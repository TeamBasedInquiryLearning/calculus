Activity 8.3.5.

Expand the following polynomial products, and then reduce to as few summands as possible.
  1. \((1-x)(1+x+x^2)\text{.}\)
  2. \((1-x)(1+x+x^2+x^3)\text{.}\)
  3. \((1-x)(1+x+x^2+x^3+x^4)\text{.}\)
  4. \((1-x)(1+x+x^2+\cdots+x^n)\text{,}\) where \(n\) is any nonnegative integer.
