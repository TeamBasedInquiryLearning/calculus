  1. \(\displaystyle \frac{27}{26}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \frac{26}{27}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \frac{27}{28}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \frac{28}{27}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \frac{29}{28}\)
