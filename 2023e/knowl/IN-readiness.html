  2. Use sigma notation for sums. (Khan Academy 1  and Active Calculus 2 )
  3. Find the area of a rectangle in the coordinate plane. (Example at Khan Academy 3 Example at virtual nerd 4 )

  5. Find the area of plane shapes, such as rectangles, triangles, circles, and trapezoids. (Math is fun 6 )
