  1. 15 inches.
  2. 7.5 inches.
  3. 30 inches.
  4. 60 inches.
(b)
What is the volume of a cylinder with radius \(r_1=15\) inches and height \(30\) inches?
(c)
What is the volume of a cylinder with radius \(r_2\) inches and height \(30\) inches?
