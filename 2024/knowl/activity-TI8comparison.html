Activity 5.8.28.

Compare \(\frac{1}{x^3+1}\) to one of the following functions where \(x>2\) and use this to determine if \(\displaystyle \int_2^\infty \frac{1}{x^3+1}dx\) is convergent or divergent.
  1. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\frac{1}{x}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\frac{1}{x^2}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\frac{1}{x^3}\)
