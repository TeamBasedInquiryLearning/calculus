What is the absolute extrema for \(f(x) = x\sqrt{4-x}\) on \([-2,4]\text{.}\)

  1. Absolute maximum is when \(x = -2\) and absolute minimum when \(x = \frac{8}{3}\text{.}\)

  2. Absolute maximum is when \(x = 4\) and absolute minimum when \(x = \frac{8}{3}\text{.}\)

  3. Absolute maximum is when \(x = \frac{8}{3}\) and absolute minimum when \(x = -2\text{.}\)

  4. Absolute maximum is when \(x = 4\) and absolute minimum when \(x = -2\text{.}\)
