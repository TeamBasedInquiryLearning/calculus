Note 2.4.10.

We have found the derivatives of \(\sin x\) and \(\cos x\text{,}\) but what about the other trigonometric functions? It turns out that the quotient rule along with some trig identities can help us! (See https://www.khanacademy.org/math/trigonometry/trig-equations-and-identities/using-trig-identities/a/trig-identity-reference for a reminder of the reciprocal and Pythagorean identities.)
