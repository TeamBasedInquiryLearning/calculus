  1. \(S_2\text{.}\)
  2. \(S_{10}\text{.}\)

    1. \(\displaystyle x_1-x_n\)
    2. \(\displaystyle x_1-x_{n+1}\)
    3. \(\displaystyle x_1-x_{n-1}\)
    4. \(\displaystyle x_1-x_n+1\)
    5. \(\displaystyle x_1-x_n-1\)
