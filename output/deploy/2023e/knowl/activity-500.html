What conclusions can you now make?
  1. \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^n \frac{1}{n}\) diverges.

  3. \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^n \frac{1}{n^2}\) converges.
  4. \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^n \frac{1}{n^2}\) diverges.
