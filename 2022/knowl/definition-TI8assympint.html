Definition 5.8.13.

For a function \(f(x)\) with a vertical asymptote at \(x=b\) and a constant \(c\text{,}\) we let \(\displaystyle \int_c^b f(x) dx\) denote

\begin{equation*} \int_c^b f(x) dx=\lim_{a\to b^{-}}\left( \int_c^a f(x) dx\right). \end{equation*}

Similarly,

\begin{equation*} \int_{b}^c f(x) dx=\lim_{a\to b^{+}}\left( \int_a^c f(x) dx\right). \end{equation*}
