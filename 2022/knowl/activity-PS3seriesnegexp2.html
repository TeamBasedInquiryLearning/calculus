Activity 9.3.6.

What function \(f(x)\) has power series \(f(x)=\displaystyle \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{x^{n+3}}{n!}=x^3+x^4+\frac{x^5}{2}+\frac{x^6}{6}+\cdots\text{?}\)

  1. \(f(x)=e^{x+3}\text{.}\)

  2. \(f(x)=e^{x^3}\text{.}\)

  3. \(f(x)=e^{3x}\text{.}\)

  4. \(f(x)=x^3e^{x}\text{.}\)

  5. \(f(x)=3e^{x}\text{.}\)
