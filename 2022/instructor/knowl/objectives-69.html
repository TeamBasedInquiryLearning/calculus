Learning Outcomes 9.

How do we use various techniques to integrate less simple functions?

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to...

  1. Approximate functions defined as power series.

  2. Determine the interval of convergence for a given power series.

  3. Compute power series by manipulating known exponential/trigonometric/binomial power series.

  4. Determine a Taylor or Maclaurin series for a function.
