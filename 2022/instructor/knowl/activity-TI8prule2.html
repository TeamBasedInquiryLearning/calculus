  1. \(\displaystyle \int_0^1 \frac{1}{x^p} dx\) converges.

  1. \(\displaystyle \int_0^1 \frac{1}{x^p} dx\) converges.

  2. \(\displaystyle \int_0^1 \frac{1}{x^p} dx\) diverges.
