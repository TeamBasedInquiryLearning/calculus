Activity 1.3.2.

Given \(f(x)=3x^2-\frac{1}{2}x+4\text{,}\) evaluate \(f(2)\) and approximate \(\displaystyle \lim_{x\to2}f(x)\) numerically (or graphically). What do you think is more likely?

  1. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \lim_{x \to 2}f(x)=f(2)\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \lim_{x \to 2}f(x) \approx f(2)\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \lim_{x \to 2}f(x) \neq f(2)\)
