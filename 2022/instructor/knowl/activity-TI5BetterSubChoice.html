Activity 5.5.7.

Consider the integral \(\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{64-9x^2}} \,dx\) once more. Suppose we still proceed using Appendix A. However, this time we choose \(u^2=x^2\) and \(a^2=64\text{.}\) Do you prefer this choice of substitution or the choice we made in Activity 5.5.6?

  1. We prefer the substitution choice of \(u^2=x^2\) and \(a^2=64\text{.}\)

  2. We prefer the substitution choice of \(u^2=9x^2\) and \(a^2=64\text{.}\)

  3. We do not have a strong preference, since these substitution choices are of the same difficulty.
