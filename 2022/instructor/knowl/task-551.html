Task 6.3.1.a.

Suppose we estimate the volume of this solid by approximating it with two cylinders of “height” \(\Delta x=3\) and radii \(f(0)=1, f(3)=4\text{:}\)

What is the volume of the cylinder with radius \(f(0)=1\text{?}\)

  1. \(\pi\cdot 0^2\cdot 3\text{.}\)

  2. \(\pi\cdot 1^2\cdot 3\text{.}\)

  3. \(\pi\cdot 3^2\cdot 3\text{.}\)

  4. \(\pi\cdot 4^2\cdot 3\text{.}\)

  5. \(\pi\cdot 6^2\cdot 3\text{.}\)

  6. \(\pi\cdot 7^2\cdot 3\text{.}\)
