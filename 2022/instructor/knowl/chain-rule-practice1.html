Activity 2.5.8.

Consider the composite function \(\displaystyle h(x) =\sqrt{e^x} = e^{\frac{x}{2}}\text{.}\) For each of the two expressions, find the derivative using the chain rule. Which of the following expressions are equal to \(h'(x)\text{?}\) Select all!

  1. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{1}{2} \left( e^x \right)^{\frac{-1}{2}} \cdot e^x \)

  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{1}{2} \left( e^x \right)^{\frac{3}{2}} \cdot e^x \)

  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{1}{2} e^{\frac{-x}{2}} \)

  4. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle e^{\frac{x}{2}} \cdot \frac{1}{2} \)

  5. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \frac{1}{2} \sqrt{e^x} \)

  6. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\sqrt{e^x} \cdot e^x \)
