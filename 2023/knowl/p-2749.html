Let \(\displaystyle\sum a_n\) be a series and suppose that \(\displaystyle\lim_{n\rightarrow\infty}\left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right|=\rho\text{.}\) Then
  1. \(\displaystyle\sum a_n\) converges if \(\rho\) is less than 1, and
  2. \(\displaystyle\sum a_n\) diverges if \(\rho\) is greater than 1.
  3. If \(\rho=1\text{,}\) we cannot determine if \(\displaystyle\sum a_n\) converges or diverges with this method.
