Observation 2.7.2.

Note that if we are taking the derivative of \(f(x)\) with respect to \(x\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation*} \frac{d}{dx}(f(x)) = f'(x). \end{equation*}
However, if we are taking the derivative of \(g(y(x))\) with respect to \(x\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation*} \frac{d}{dx}(g(y)) = g'(y) \cdot \frac{dy}{dx}. \end{equation*}
