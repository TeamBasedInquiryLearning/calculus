Activity 5.8.12.

Does \(\displaystyle\int_1^\infty \frac{1}{\sqrt x} dx\) converge or diverge?
  1. Converges because \(\displaystyle\lim_{b\to 0^+}\left[2\sqrt b-2\right]\) converges.
  2. Diverges because \(\displaystyle\lim_{b\to 0^+}\left[2\sqrt b-2\right]\) diverges.
  3. Converges because \(\displaystyle\lim_{b\to \infty}\left[2\sqrt b-2\right]\) converges.
  4. Diverges because \(\displaystyle\lim_{b\to \infty}\left[2\sqrt b-2\right]\) diverges.
