Activity 5.8.14.

Which of these limits is equal to \(\displaystyle\int_0^1 \frac{1}{\sqrt x} dx\text{?}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \lim_{a\to0^+}\int_a^1\frac{1}{\sqrt x}dx\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \lim_{a\to0^+}\left[2\sqrt x\right]_a^1\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \lim_{a\to0^+}\left[2-2\sqrt a\right]\)

  4. All of these.
