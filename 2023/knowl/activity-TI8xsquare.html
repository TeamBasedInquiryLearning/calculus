Activity 5.8.16.

Does \(\displaystyle\int_0^1 \frac{1}{x^2} dx\) converge or diverge?

  1. Converges because \(\displaystyle\lim_{a\to 0^+}\left[-1+\frac{1}{a}\right]\) converges.

  2. Diverges because \(\displaystyle\lim_{a\to 0^+}\left[-1+\frac{1}{a}\right]\) diverges.

  3. Converges because \(\displaystyle\lim_{a\to 1^-}\left[-1+\frac{1}{a}\right]\) converges.

  4. Diverges because \(\displaystyle\lim_{a\to 1^-}\left[-1+\frac{1}{a}\right]\) diverges.
