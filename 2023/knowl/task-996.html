Task 9.3.5.b.

Evaluating \(a=2x\text{,}\) what is the power series for \(\exp(a)=\exp(2x)=f(x)\text{?}\)

  1. \(\exp(2x)=\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{2x^n}{n!}=2+2x+x^2+\frac{1}{3}x^3+\dots\text{.}\)

  2. \(\exp(2x)=\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^\infty 2x\frac{x^n}{n!}=2x+2x^2+x^3+\frac{1}{3}x^4+\dots\text{.}\)

  3. \(\exp(2x)=\displaystyle\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(2x)^n}{n!}=1+2x+2x^2+\frac{4}{3}x^3+\dots\text{.}\)
