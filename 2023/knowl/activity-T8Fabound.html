Activity 5.8.10.

Which of these limits is equal to \(\displaystyle\int_1^\infty \frac{1}{x^2} dx\text{?}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \lim_{b\to\infty}\int_1^b\frac{1}{x^2}dx\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \lim_{b\to\infty}\left[-\frac{1}{x}\right]_1^b\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \lim_{b\to\infty}\left[-\frac{1}{b}+1\right]\)

  4. All of these.
